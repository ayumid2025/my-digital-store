Features for My Digital Store:
- Users can sign up with a username and password.
- Users can log in and log out.
- Sellers can upload e-books and songs (with title, author, price, and file).
- Buyers can see a list of all items for sale.
- Buyers can click on an item to see more details.
- Buyers can buy an item (for free at first).
- After buying, buyers can download the file.
- Buyers can search for items by title.
- Buyers can leave reviews and ratings.
- Sellers can edit or delete their items.
